Plastic waste

Plastic waste, or plastic pollution, is the accumulation of plastic objects that isn't recycled and ends up in the landfill or thrown into unregulated dump sites.

3.3 million tonnes of plastic produced per year.
2.8 million tonnes of waste goes to landfills every year.
15 billion plastic bags and 57 million straws are used every day.

Types of Plastic Waste in Businesses/Households

  • Bags
  • Straws
